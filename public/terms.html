Client Obligations

By signing the rental contract, the client commits to:

  • Use the vehicle responsibly and according to traffic laws
  • Not use the vehicle for illegal purposes or racing
  • Not sublease the vehicle to third parties
  • Perform regular maintenance checks during the rental period
  • Return the vehicle on time as specified in the contract
  • Return the vehicle with the same fuel level as at pickup
  • Pay all fines and violations during the rental period
