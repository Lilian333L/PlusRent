Moldova Traffic Law & Road Safety

Why is a sober driver important? Because penalties for drunk driving in Moldova are severe — far more expensive than our service.

Art. 233 para. (1) — Driving Under the Influence of Alcohol

Under the Contravention Code of the Republic of Moldova, driving a motor vehicle with a blood alcohol concentration above 0.3 g/l (or 0.15 mg/l in exhaled air) is punishable by:

  • Fine of 700 to 1000 conventional units (approximately 35,000 – 50,000 MDL)
  • Or administrative arrest of 10–15 days
  • In both cases — driving licence suspension for 6 months to 3 years

Art. 233 para. (2) — Handing Over the Car to an Intoxicated Person

Warning: knowingly handing over the driving of a vehicle to a person under the influence of alcohol is punishable the same as driving under the influence — fine of 35,000–50,000 MDL, arrest of 10–15 days and licence suspension for 6 months – 3 years. If your friend has been drinking, don't let them drive — call +373 60 000 500.

Art. 233 para. (4) — Driving Without a Licence or with a Suspended Licence

Driving under the influence of alcohol by a person without a driving licence or with a previously suspended licence is punishable by a fine of 40,000 – 65,000 MDL (800–1300 c.u.) or administrative arrest of 10–15 days.

Legal Blood Alcohol Limit (BAC)

  • 0.3 g/l — blood alcohol concentration
  • 0.15 mg/l — alcohol vapours in exhaled air

Any value above these limits constitutes a contravention. Even a glass of wine at dinner can exceed the threshold, depending on body weight and time elapsed.
