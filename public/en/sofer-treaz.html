LEGISLATION & SAFETY

Why a Sober Driver Matters

Penalties for drunk driving in Moldova are severe — far more expensive than our service.

Article 233 (1) — Driving Under the Influence

Under the Contraventional Code of the Republic of Moldova, driving with a blood alcohol level above 0.3 g/L is punishable by:

  • Fine 700-1000 conventional units ≈ 35,000-50,000 MDL
  • Or administrative arrest 10-15 days
  • License suspension 6 months — 3 years

Article 233 (2) — Handing Over the Car to an Intoxicated Person

Warning: if you give the keys to a drunk friend, the same penalty applies — fine 35,000-50,000 MDL, arrest 10-15 days, license suspension 6 months — 3 years. If your friend has been drinking, call +373 60 000 500.

Permitted Alcohol Limit (BAC)

  • 0.3 g/L in blood
  • 0.15 mg/L in exhaled air

Even one glass of wine at dinner can exceed the limit, depending on weight and time elapsed.
