Car Rental Chisinau — PlusRent

PlusRent is the car rental service in Chisinau and across Moldova with 24/7 delivery to Chisinau International Airport (KIV). Prices from €15/day, third-party liability insurance (RCA) included, no hidden costs. The fleet includes BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Volkswagen, Toyota — all technically inspected and sanitised before every handover.

We offer car rental for a day, a week or long-term with substantial discounts. Free delivery to Chisinau Airport during working hours, pickup and return at different locations (Chisinau, Airport, Iasi). Booking takes under 2 minutes.

Round-the-clock customer support at +373 60 000 500 — technical assistance, booking changes, route advice. All cars include RCA insurance, air conditioning and GPS. Payment at the end — cash (MDL/EUR) or bank card, no deposit at booking.

Frequently asked questions

Short and direct answers — for any other questions, call us at +373 60 000 500.

What are PlusRent's car rental prices?

Prices start from €15/day for Economy class, €30/day for Standard and €60–€120/day for Premium (BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Porsche). All rates include third-party liability insurance (RCA), 200 km/day mileage and 24/7 support. No deposit at booking — pay when you pick up the car.

Do you deliver cars to Chisinau Airport?

Yes. Delivery to Chisinau International Airport (KIV) is free (€0) during working hours 08:00–18:00. Outside working hours a +€15 surcharge applies per pickup/return. The service is available 24/7.

What documents are required to rent a car?

You need: a valid driving licence, ID card or passport, and a security deposit. Minimum age is 21 — driving experience matters more than age. For foreign citizens we accept international passport and an International Driving Permit if your national licence is not in Latin script. All data is confidential.

What is the minimum rental period?

The minimum rental period is 24 hours. The included mileage allowance is 200 km per day. For bookings of 7+ days or long-term rentals we offer substantial discounts — ask at +373 60 000 500.

What cars are available to rent?

The PlusRent fleet includes: BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Volkswagen, Hyundai, Honda, Toyota. Three categories: Economy (€15+), Standard (€30+) and Premium (€60+). All cars are technically inspected, sanitised and equipped with air conditioning + GPS.

Are there any hidden fees or extra costs?

No. The price you see is the price you pay. Possible transparent surcharges: Chisinau Airport delivery FREE (€0), Iasi Airport +€175, pickup/return outside 08:00–18:00 +€15. Third-party liability insurance (RCA) is included in all rates.

Do you deliver to Iasi Airport (Romania)?

Yes. PlusRent offers delivery to Iasi International Airport (IAS), Romania, for a €175 fee. The Chisinau–Iasi distance is about 3 hours by road, and our driver assists with the border crossing. One-way rentals are available: pickup in Chisinau, return in Iasi (or vice versa). Convenient for international flights with Wizz Air, Ryanair, TAROM. Call +373 60 000 500 to coordinate the exact time.

How do I book a car?

Book online at plusrent.md in under 2 minutes or call +373 60 000 500. We contact you within 24 hours for confirmation. We accept cash (MDL/EUR), bank card or bank transfer — payment when you pick up the car.
