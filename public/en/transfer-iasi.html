Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to most asked questions.

How much does Iași - Chișinău transfer cost? +

We have 3 tiers: Standard €140, Business €175 (most chosen), VIP €230. Plus the Concierge package €195 with meet & greet included. All prices are all-inclusive — border, WiFi, water and luggage assistance included.

How long is the journey Iași - Chișinău? +

Distance is 130 km. The actual drive takes 2-2.5 hours. Plus 30-60 minutes at Sculeni-Albița border. Total: about 3-3.5 hours. Our drivers know optimal routes and handle customs formalities quickly.

How many passengers can travel? +

Standard configuration: 6+1 seats (6 passengers + driver). Price is for the entire vehicle, not per person. Whether you travel alone or with 6 — cost stays the same. For larger groups (more than 6 passengers), call us at +373 60 000 500, WhatsApp or Telegram — we arrange multi-vehicle solutions. Luggage included, no limits.

How does border crossing work? +

Main point is Sculeni-Albița. Valid passport required. Process takes 30-60 minutes on average. Our experienced drivers assist with formalities, explain process, help with documents if needed. Border assistance included in price.

Can I book round-trip? +

Yes. We offer round-trip transfers with 4-8 hour wait. Iași Airport ↔ Chișinău round-trip: €260 (Standard), €310 (Business), €400 (VIP). Round-trip gets automatic 10% discount.

How do I pay? +

No advance payment for individual bookings. Pay the driver at trip end — cash (EUR or MDL) or card. For corporate and multiple bookings we offer monthly invoicing options.

Do drivers speak English? +

All our drivers speak Romanian and Russian. English is available on request — please mention this when booking. For VIP Executive transfers, multilingual drivers are included by default.

How can I contact you? +

Multiple ways: Phone +373 60 000 500 (24/7), WhatsApp at the same number, Telegram @plusrent, Email support@plusrent.md, or use the callback form on our website. Response time: 3-5 minutes during business hours.
