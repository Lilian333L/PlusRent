Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How much does Iași - Chișinău transfer cost? +

3 tiers: Standard €140, Business €175 (most chosen), VIP €230. Plus Concierge €195. All all-inclusive — border, WiFi, water, luggage included.

How long is the journey? +

Distance is 130 km. Drive: 2-2.5 hours. Plus 30-60 minutes at Sculeni-Albița border. Total: about 3-3.5 hours.

How many passengers can travel? +

Standard: 6+1 seats. Price for entire vehicle. For larger groups, contact us via phone, WhatsApp or Telegram — we arrange multi-vehicle solutions.

How does border crossing work? +

Main point is Sculeni-Albița. Valid passport required. Process takes 30-60 minutes. Drivers help with formalities. Border assistance included.

Can I book round-trip? +

Yes. Round-trip with 4-8h wait: €260/€310/€400 by tier. Automatic 10% discount.

How do I pay? +

No advance payment. Pay at end — cash (EUR/MDL) or card. Corporate accounts: monthly invoicing.

How can I contact you? +

Multiple ways: Phone +373 60 000 500, WhatsApp, Telegram @plusrent, Viber, Email. Response: 3-5 minutes. Click the floating contact button (bottom right) anytime.
