ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

How much does a personal driver cost per day?+
Two options. On your car: Standard 2,000 MDL/day (8h), Business 2,800 MDL (10h), VIP 4,000 MDL (12h). On our cars (all-inclusive 100 km): Standard 3,500 MDL, Business 5,500 MDL (Audi A6/Lexus), VIP Executive 8,500 MDL (Mercedes E/BMW 5).
What's the difference between the two options?+
Option A: we bring only the driver — ideal if you have a car you love. Option B: we bring a premium vehicle + driver, all-inclusive (100 km, snacks, water) — ideal when you want premium status without ownership.
Who are your drivers?+
Verified professionals with minimum 5 years' experience, in uniform (suit for Business+ and VIP), multilingual (EN/RO/RU), discreet, punctual. All hold valid licences, clean records and professional insurance.
Can I have the same driver every time?+
Yes. Same-driver option is available for recurring contracts. The driver learns your routes and preferences. For Corporate and VIP tiers — same driver is standard.
Is there a minimum booking?+
Minimum 4 hours = 1,200 MDL for short bookings. Standard packages are 8h, 10h and 12h. For monthly or corporate — no minimum, custom pricing.
Are there discounts for longer contracts?+
Yes:
  • 5+ days: −10%
  • 10+ days: −15%
  • Monthly contract: −20%
  • Corporate retainer: up to −20% + priority booking, account manager, monthly invoicing, free upgrades
How do I pay?+
Individual bookings — pay at the end, cash (MDL/EUR) or card. Monthly and corporate — monthly invoicing (B2B friendly). No advance payment for individuals.
Can I have a driver 24/7 on-call?+
Yes. VIP On-Call option gives you 24/7 availability — ideal for executives and expats with irregular schedules. Premium rate, maximum flexibility. Contact us for details.
How can I contact you?+
Multiple channels: WhatsApp, Telegram @plusrent, Viber, Phone +373 60 000 500, email support@plusrent.md. Response in 3–5 minutes. Click the contact button (bottom right) anytime.
