ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

How much does a personal driver cost per day? +
Two options. On your car: Standard 2,000 MDL/day (8h), Business 2,800 MDL (10h), VIP 4,000 MDL (12h). On our cars (all-inclusive 100km): Standard 3,500 MDL, Business 5,500 MDL, VIP Executive 8,500 MDL.
What's the difference between the two options? +
Option A: we bring only the driver, drives your car — ideal if you already have a car you love. Option B: we bring premium car + driver, all-inclusive (100km included, snacks, water) — ideal when you want premium vehicle status without ownership hassle.
Who are your drivers? +
Our drivers are verified professionals with minimum 5 years experience, in uniform (suit for Business+ and VIP), multilingual (RO/RU/EN), discreet, punctual. All have valid licenses, clean records, professional insurance, executive driving training.
Can I have the same driver every time? +
Yes, we offer same driver option for recurring contracts or personal preferences. Driver learns your routes, music preferences, favorite paths. For Corporate and VIP — same driver standard.
Is there a minimum booking? +
Minimum 4 hours = 1,200 MDL for short bookings. Standard packages are 8h, 10h, 12h. For monthly or corporate — no minimum, custom pricing.
Are there discounts for longer contracts? +
Yes:
  • 5+ days: -10%
  • 10+ days: -15%
  • Monthly contract: -20%
  • Corporate retainer: up to -20% + benefits (priority booking, dedicated account manager, monthly invoicing, free upgrades)
How do I pay? +
For individual bookings — pay at end, cash (MDL/EUR) or card. For monthly and corporate — monthly invoicing (B2B friendly). No advance payment for individuals.
Can I have a driver 24/7 on-call? +
Yes, VIP On-Call option (24/7 availability). Special package for executives, dignitaries, expats traveling irregularly. Premium rate but maximum flexibility. Call for details.
How can I contact you? +
Multiple ways: Phone +373 60 000 500, WhatsApp, Telegram @plusrent, Viber, Email. Response: 3-5 minutes. Click the floating contact button (bottom right) anytime.
