Frequently Asked Questions about Car Rental

Quick answers to the most common questions about our service.

How much does car rental cost in Chișinău? +

Prices at PlusRent start from €15/day for Economy category, €31/day for Standard and €61/day for Premium (BMW, Mercedes, Audi). All prices include third-party liability insurance and 24/7 support. For long-term rentals (7+ days) discounts up to 20% apply.

What documents do I need to rent a car? +

You need: valid driver's license and passport or ID card. Each case is evaluated individually — driving experience matters more than age. For foreign citizens we accept international passport and international driving permit if the national license is not in EU format.

Can I rent a car at Chișinău Airport? +

Yes, we offer 24/7 delivery to Chișinău Airport. During business hours (8:00-18:00) delivery is free. Outside business hours a fee of +€15 applies. We also deliver to Iași Airport (Romania), just 2.5 hours from Chișinău — fee +€35.

Is insurance included? +

Yes, all PlusRent rentals include third-party liability insurance (civil liability) in the displayed price. 24/7 roadside assistance is also included. No hidden costs.

Is there a minimum age for rental? +

Each case is evaluated individually. At PlusRent driving experience matters more than age. Contact us for confirmation at +373 60 000 500 — we find solutions for most clients.

Can I pay by card or only cash? +

We accept cash (EUR, MDL, USD), bank card (Visa, MasterCard), bank transfer and online payment at booking. Payment can be made upon delivery or in advance.

How many kilometers are included in the rental? +

Each rental includes a daily limit of 200 km. For exact total mileage calculation, multiply 200 km × number of rental days. For example: 3 days = 600 km, 7 days = 1400 km. Additional kilometers are charged per extra km.

Can I drive the car outside of Moldova? +

Yes, international trips are allowed with prior notification. We permit travel to Romania, Ukraine and EU countries. Some destinations may have additional requirements. Let us know at booking which countries you plan to visit.

How is the booking made? +

Online booking takes 2 minutes on plusrent.md: choose the car, select dates, fill in details and confirm. Or call +373 60 000 500 — we answer 24/7. You will be contacted for confirmation within 24 hours.

What car brands are available? +

The PlusRent fleet includes BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Volkswagen, Skoda, Toyota, Hyundai, Honda, Porsche and others. We have sedans, SUVs, crossovers and hatchbacks, with automatic or manual transmission, gasoline/diesel/hybrid.

Is there a security deposit? +

Yes, a refundable security deposit applies which guarantees the safe return of the car. The deposit is returned in full if the car is returned without damage, all traffic fines are paid, and the car is returned on time with the correct fuel level.

What do I do in case of accident or breakdown? +

Contact us immediately at +373 60 000 500. 24/7 roadside assistance is included in all rentals. We will guide you through the procedure, send a replacement if needed and coordinate with insurance.

Can I cancel or modify the booking? +

Yes, changes can be made in advance with prior notification. For cancellations and modifications contact us at +373 60 000 500 or support@plusrent.md at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you also offer sober driver service? +

Yes, we offer Sober Driver service in Chișinău 24/7 from 150 MDL — details at plusrent.md/en/sofer-treaz. Arrival time 15-20 minutes. We drive your car home safely.

Can I pick up and return the car in different locations? +

Yes, we offer 3 locations: Chișinău Airport, Our Office (Meșterul Manole 20) and Iași Airport. You can pick up from one and return to another. Delivery to Chișinău Airport is free during business hours. An additional fee applies for Iași Airport (+€35).
