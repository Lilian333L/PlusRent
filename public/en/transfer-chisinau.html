Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How much does Chișinău Airport transfer cost? +

3 tiers Airport → Center: Standard 800 MDL, Business 1,000 MDL (most popular), VIP 1,400 MDL. Plus Concierge €195 with full service. All all-inclusive.

How long does the transfer take? +

Airport → Center: 20-30 min (depending on traffic). Other zones (Botanica/Buiucani): 25-35 min. Other Moldovan cities: from 1 hour. Driver picks optimal route.

How are vehicles selected for my group? +

Vehicle is matched to your tier and group size. Volume of vehicles for groups is discussed by phone — call +373 60 000 500 or message via WhatsApp/Telegram. We arrange single vehicle or multi-vehicle solutions based on passengers, luggage, and special requirements.

What if my flight is delayed? +

No problem. We monitor your flight automatically. Standard: 30 min wait included. Business+/VIP: 60 min wait included. Driver waits without extra charges.

How do I pay? +

No advance payment for individual bookings. Pay driver at end — cash (MDL/EUR) or card. Corporate accounts: monthly invoicing.

Do drivers speak English? +

All drivers speak Romanian and Russian. English available on request — mention this when booking. For VIP — multilingual included by default.

How can I contact you? +

Multiple ways: Phone +373 60 000 500, WhatsApp, Telegram @plusrent, Viber, Email. Response: 3-5 minutes. Click the floating contact button (bottom right) anytime.
