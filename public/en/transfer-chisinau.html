FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

Quick answers before you book.

How much does a Chisinau Airport — Chisinau transfer cost?+
Chisinau Airport — Chisinau City Center transfer: Standard €40 (800 MDL), Business Comfort €50 (1,000 MDL), VIP Executive €70 (1,400 MDL). All prices are all-inclusive — WiFi, water, luggage assistance, wait time included. Distance 13–15 km, duration 25–30 minutes.
What is included in the transfer price?+
All transfers include: premium car (Audi A6 / Lexus / Mercedes depending on the package), professional multilingual driver (EN/RO/RU), 4G WiFi, bottled water, USB chargers, luggage assistance, 30 min free wait (60 min for VIP). No hidden fees.
How far in advance should I book?+
Recommended at least 4–6 hours in advance to secure your preferred vehicle. For urgent bookings (under 2 hours), availability is limited — please call directly: +373 60 000 500. Service available non-stop, 24/7, including holidays.
Do you offer transfers from other Moldovan cities?+
Yes, we cover all of Moldova. Transfer to Chisinau Airport from: Balti from €100 (2,000 MDL), Cahul from €120 (2,400 MDL), Comrat from €80 (1,600 MDL), Soroca from €110 (2,200 MDL), Orhei from €40 (800 MDL). For special routes, please call.
How many passengers can travel?+
Our vehicles have 4+1 seats. The price is per vehicle, not per person — whether you travel alone or with 6 people, the cost is the same. Luggage is unlimited (up to 6 standard suitcases). No extra charges.
How does Meet & Greet work at the airport?+
On Business and VIP, the driver greets you in the terminal holding a sign with your name, helps with your luggage and walks you directly to the vehicle. On Standard, the driver waits in the airport parking lot and texts you the exact location upon landing.
How do I pay for the transfer?+
No advance payment for individual bookings. You pay the driver at the end of the ride — cash (MDL or EUR) or bank card. For corporations and recurring bookings, we offer monthly invoicing options. The fixed price is guaranteed.
What happens if my flight is delayed?+
We monitor flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, the driver waits at no extra charge — 30 min included on Standard, 60 min on Business & VIP. After that: 50 MDL per additional 15 minutes.

About Our Transfer Airport Chisinau Service

PlusRent is Moldova's premium airport transfer provider, operating since 2022, headquartered in Chisinau (20 Mesterul Manole st.). Our service covers Chisinau International Airport (IATA code: KIV), located 13–15 km from the city center — about 25–30 minutes by car. Rates start from €40 (800 MDL) per vehicle, not per person, for the airport–city center route. Our active fleet includes Audi A6 2022, Lexus NX300h and Mercedes E-Class. All drivers are professional multilingual specialists (English, Romanian, Russian) wearing uniforms. The service operates non-stop (24/7), including public holidays. No advance payment required — payment is made on arrival, in cash (MDL/EUR) or by bank card.
